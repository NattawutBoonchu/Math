🔢 Integers & Directed Numbers

Integers include all whole numbers: positive, negative, and zero.

  • Adding a negative = subtracting: 5 + (−3) = 2
  • Subtracting a negative = adding: 5 − (−3) = 8
  • Same signs → positive | Different signs → negative
📌 Examples
−4 + 7 = 3
6 − (−2) = 8
(−3) × (−4) = 12
💡 Tip: Picture a number line — right = add, left = subtract!

🔢 Fractions, Decimals & Percentages

All three represent parts of a whole and can be converted.

  • Fraction → Decimal: Divide numerator by denominator
  • Decimal → %: Multiply by 100
  • % → Fraction: Write over 100 and simplify
📌 Examples
3/4 = 0.75 = 75%
40% = 40/100 = 2/5
💡 Remember: "Per cent" means per hundred!

🔢 Ratios & Proportions

A ratio compares two quantities. Simplify like a fraction.

  • 3 to 5 → 3:5
  • 6:9 simplifies to 2:3
📌 Divide $40 in ratio 3:5
Total parts = 3 + 5 = 8
1 part = $40 ÷ 8 = $5
Share A = $15 | Share B = $25

📐 Introduction to Algebra

  • Expression: 3x + 5 (no equals sign)
  • Equation: 3x + 5 = 14 (has equals sign)
  • Only like terms can be combined (same variable)
📌 Simplify
4x + 3x = 7x
5y − 2y + 3 = 3y + 3
💡 Tip: 3x and 3y are NOT like terms!

📐 Solving Equations

Use inverse operations — do the same to both sides.

📌 Two-Step: 2x + 3 = 11
2x = 11 − 3 = 8
x = 8 ÷ 2 = 4
💡 Golden Rule: Whatever you do to one side, do to the other!

📐 Number Patterns

  • 3, 6, 9 … → Rule: 3n
  • 5, 8, 11 … → Rule: 3n + 2
📌 10th term of 3n + 2
3(10) + 2 = 32

📏 Angle Facts

  • Straight line = 180°
  • Full turn = 360°
  • Triangle angles sum = 180°
  • Vertically opposite angles are equal
📌 Triangle: 55°, 70°, x?
x = 180 − 55 − 70 = 55°

📏 Area & Perimeter

  • Rectangle: A = l×w | P = 2(l+w)
  • Triangle: A = ½×b×h
  • Circle: A = πr² | C = 2πr
📌 Triangle: b=8, h=5
A = ½ × 8 × 5 = 20 cm²
💡 Units! Area → cm² | Length → cm

📏 Volume of Prisms

V = Area of cross-section × length

📌 Box: 5×3×4
V = 60 cm³

📊 Measures of Centre & Spread

  • Mean: Sum ÷ count
  • Median: Middle value when sorted
  • Mode: Most frequent value
  • Range: Max − Min
📌 Dataset: 4, 7, 2, 9, 7, 5
Mean = 34 ÷ 6 ≈ 5.7
Median = (5+7)÷2 = 6
Mode = 7 | Range = 7

📊 Probability

P(event) = favourable outcomes ÷ total outcomes

  • 0 = impossible | 1 = certain
  • P(not A) = 1 − P(A)
📌 P(rolling a 3)
P(3) = 1/6 ≈ 16.7%
💡 Tip: Can be written as fraction, decimal, or percentage!
